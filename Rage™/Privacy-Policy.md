# Privacy Policy for Rage™

**Effective Date:** 11 January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Rage™** ("the Bot", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our services on the Discord platform. By using Rage™, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

Rage™ may collect and process the following information strictly for functionality and operational purposes.

### 1.1 User Information
- Discord User ID  
- Username, avatar, and banner  
- User status and activity (as permitted by Discord APIs)  
- Commands executed and interactions with the Bot  

### 1.2 Server (Guild) Information
- Guild ID and guild name  
- Guild owner ID  
- Roles and permissions  
- Channels and categories  
- Guild icon and banner  
- Member count  
- Bot join and leave logs  
- Pre-generated or fetched invite links (if required for functionality)

> Rage™ does **not** collect passwords, email addresses, or private Discord messages.

## 2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

- Providing core bot features and services

- Monitoring abuse, misuse, or violations  
- Maintaining security and platform integrity  

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade user data.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information only in the following cases:

- With Discord, as required for bot operation
